# Race to the King 2025 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 21/06/2025 | 15:10:28 | 64mi (100km)

---

## Race Overview
- Race to the King 2025
- Organised by Threshold Sports (Threshold Trail Series)
- Saturday 21st June 2025, 6:30am start
- Start: West Dean Estate (near Chichester)
- Finish: Winchester (King Alfred's statue)
- Options: Castle 50k or full 100k
- 1,073 competitors
- South Downs Way National Trail

## 2025 Course
- Ancient paths through the South Downs
- Rolling hills and coastal views
- Winchester to Chichester route (reversed for some distances)
- Breathtaking South Downs scenery
