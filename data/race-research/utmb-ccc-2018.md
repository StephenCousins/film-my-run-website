# UTMB CCC 2018 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 31/08/2018 | 23:57:30 | 62mi (101km)

---

## Race Overview
- CCC = Courmayeur (Italy) - Champex-Lac (Switzerland) - Chamonix (France)
- 101km with 6100m of elevation gain
- Point-to-point course starting in Courmayeur, ending in Chamonix
- Follows the last part of the UTMB route
- Starts same morning as UTMB, at 9am

## 2018 Course Reroute
- Course was REROUTED because of a rockfall event earlier in the week
- A hiker died in the rockfall
- Organisers added another climb to approximately equal the original elevation gain
- Result: last section became a series of shorter (by UTMB standards) intense climbs
- Followed by short stretches of level singletrack or technical downhill

## 2018 Weather Conditions
- At the start in Courmayeur: bright and clear, partially overcast, no rain
- Opposite conditions to Chamonix
- Warnings before start indicated crossing into France would bring worse weather
- For TDS that same week: warnings about "apocalyptic weather" that seemed overblown at first

## Race Character
- Often called "The Little Swiss Sister of UTMB"
- Tackling ~20,000 feet of gain in 100k
- Three countries in one race

## Context for Stephen
- 23:57:30 finish - just under 24 hours
- First UTMB event
- Dealt with the course reroute and changing conditions
