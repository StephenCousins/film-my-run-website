# UTMB 100 Mile 2023 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 01/09/2023 | 44:47:11 | 110mi

---

## Race Overview
- Started 6pm CEST, Friday September 1, 2023
- 170km (106.2 miles) with 10,000m (32,687 feet) of climbing
- Circumnavigates the Mont-Blanc massif

## Weather & Course Modifications
- Route was modified due to weather conditions
- Col des Montets section changed - runners headed via Béchar and Bois de la Trappe instead (a variation planned for bad weather)

## Men's Race
- **Winner:** Jim Walmsley (USA/Hoka) - 19:37:43 (NEW COURSE RECORD)
- First American male to win UTMB 100
- 2nd: Zach Miller (USA/The North Face) - 19:58:58
- 3rd: Germain Grangier (France/The North Face) - 20:10:52

Kilian Jornet (previous year's champion and record holder) was absent due to injury.

The first half was a heated battle between Walmsley, Miller, and a close group. Around km 140, Walmsley shifted into another gear and pulled away. By La Flégère aid station after the final climb, the race was his.

## Women's Race
- **Winner:** Courtney Dauwalter - dominated the women's field

## Context for Stephen
- You finished in 44:47:11 - over double the winner's time
- This was the big one - UTMB 100, the most prestigious ultra in the world
- Course record was set this year by Walmsley
