# Goodwood Marathon 2020 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 06/12/2020 | 02:58:57 | 26.2mi (FIRST SUB-3!)

---

## Race Overview
- RunThrough Running Grand Prix at Goodwood Motor Circuit
- Smooth, traffic-free tarmac motor racing circuit
- Located at the foot of the Sussex Downs
- Multiple laps around the racetrack

## COVID Context
- December 2020 - during pandemic restrictions
- COVID safety measures "really top notch"
- All marshals wore masks, everyone keeping apart
- Minimal contact (no bag drop for example)
- Felt significant after months of cancelled events
- "It felt nice to be doing something like this again"

## 2020 Weather Conditions
- FREEZING cold
- One runner: "in a bit of a bad way at the end, had to dress pretty sharpish, as it was freezing"
- Runners kept masks on in race village mainly because it was so cold
- Windy - front runners "seemed to glide along despite the wind"

## Course Character
- Flat motor racing circuit
- Full of runners all going round
- Weaving required
- 2:44 considered "an amazing run" given the conditions (wind, weaving)

## Context for Stephen
- **02:58:57 - YOUR FIRST SUB-3 MARATHON!**
- Achieved in freezing, windy conditions
- On a motor racing circuit during COVID
- Major milestone after years of ultras

## Significance
- First time breaking the 3-hour barrier
- Proved marathon speed despite being primarily an ultra runner
- Would go on to run even faster (2:55:42 Paris 2023)
