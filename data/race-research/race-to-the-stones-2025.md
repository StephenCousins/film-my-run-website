## Race Overview
- Race to the Stones (RTTS) 2025 - Edition 12
- Organised by Threshold Sports (Threshold Trail Series)
- 12th-13th July 2025
- 2,000+ competitors
- 100km along the Ridgeway - "Britain's oldest road"
- Start: Near the Uffington White Horse (Oxfordshire)
- Finish: Avebury Stone Circle (Wiltshire)
- Options: 100k non-stop, Ridgeline 50k
- Â£1,000 course record prize on offer

## 2025 Weather Conditions
- HOT conditions
- **Race start moved to 5am** due to heat (later than originally planned)
- Allowed runners to take advantage of cooler morning temperatures
- Extreme conditions for 100k running
- Unfavourable conditions for record attempts
