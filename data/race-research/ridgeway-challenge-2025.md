# Ridgeway Challenge 2025 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 31/05/2025 | 24:23:36 | 86mi (139km)

---

## Race Overview
- The Ridgeway Challenge 2025
- Organised by Runaway Racing
- Saturday 31st May 2025
- 86 miles (139km) with 1,900m elevation gain
- 28-hour cutoff
- Start: Ivinghoe Beacon (near Tring)
- Finish: Avebury Stone Circle
- First held in 2004
- UTMB Index race
- 115 finishers recorded

## Course Character
- Follows The Ridgeway National Trail
- 5,000-year-old ancient trackway
- Through the Chiltern Hills and North Wessex Downs
- Historic route with stunning views
- Mix of trail and track surfaces
- Rolling terrain with moderate elevation
