## Race Overview
- Winter on the Downs 55 miles
- Organised by UK Ultra
- Saturday 7th December 2024
- Start/Finish: Eastbourne
- 55 miles (89km) with 2,286m (7,700ft) elevation gain
- UTMB Index race
- Part of the South Downs Triple Crown series
- Single-loop course through East and West Sussex

## Course Character
- Generally very runnable - "rolling hills with some longer, steeper climbs"
- 7,700ft of elevation across 55 miles
- Iconic South Downs scenery including:
  - Beachy Head
  - Seven Sisters
- Trail running through chalk downland
- December conditions - short daylight hours

## Storm Darragh Context
- Storm Darragh hit UK on 6-7 December 2024 (race day!)
- Named by Met Office on December 5th
- Red weather warning for parts of UK
- 96mph gusts recorded at Berry Head (Devon)
- 93mph gusts at Capel Curig (North Wales)
- Race conditions likely severely affected by storm
