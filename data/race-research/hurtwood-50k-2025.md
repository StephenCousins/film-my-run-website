## Race Overview

- UTMB Index race
- 366 finishers
- 10-hour cutoff
- SportIdent timing with live leaderboard

## Course Character
- Follows the Greensand Way into the forested Surrey Hills
- Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty (AONB)
- "Lollipop" route - essentially out and back with a 5-mile loop
- Route passes over:
  - Leith Hill (highest point in southeast England)
  - Holmbury Hill
  - Pitch Hill
- Well-marked, easy to follow trails
- Woodlands, ridgelines, and valleys of The Hurtwood
- Mix of terrain typical of Surrey Hills

## Aid Stations
- Regular checkpoints with food and drinks
- Miles 7, 13, 18, and 24
- Well-supported for a trail ultra

## Race Character
- "Classic late-season Southern Ultra"
- Fast and exciting out-and-back route
- 5-mile loop adds variety mid-race
- December timing makes it a popular end-of-season target
- Good weather for this time of year can make it fast
- Muddy if wet - Surrey clay
