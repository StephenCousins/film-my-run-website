# UTS 50k by UTMB 2022 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 01/07/2022 | 11:16:17 | 32mi (50km)

---

## Race Overview
- Ultra-Trail Snowdonia by UTMB - UTS 50
- 50km with 3,100m elevation gain
- Held in Llanberis, Wales (around Snowdon)
- First year as part of the new UTMB World Series
- 593 finishers in the 50k distance
- Over 1,000 runners from 40+ countries across all distances
- Started at National Slate Museum, Llanberis

## 2022 Course Character
- Technically challenging, mountainous terrain
- Very rough underfoot with long sections of rocky terrain
- Can be very slippery when wet
- Very little soft ground or tarmac
- Short sections of easy scrambling with some exposure
- May need to use hands to steady yourself in places

## 2022 Results

### Men's 50k
- **Winner:** Jack Scott - 5:32:26
