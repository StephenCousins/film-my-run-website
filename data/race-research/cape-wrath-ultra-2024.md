## Race Overview
- Scotland's premier multi-stage ultra running event
- 400km over 8 days
- Fort William to Cape Wrath
- Winds through Knoydart, Kintail, Torridon, Assynt, and Sutherland
- "The world's most inspirational landscapes"
- 5 days are true 'ultra marathons' over mountainous and often rough terrain

## 2024 Conditions

### Weather
- First two days: warm and sunny
- Dry conditions underfoot helped but heat took its toll
- Down to 141 runners after just two days

### THE NOROVIRUS
- 2024 edition "will be remembered for the vomiting bug"
- Challenge was "significantly compounded by this illness"
- Many runners affected

### Completion Statistics
- Starters: 172
- Ultra Finishers: 57 (33%) - DOWN from 51% in 2023
- Explorer Finishers: 68 (39%)
- Overall Completions: 125 (72%)

The low finish rate was attributed to the Norovirus impact.

## Key Stages
- **Day 3:** Deemed the toughest - 68km and over 2,300m ascent from Kinloch Hourn to Achnashellach
- **Day 4:** Shorter but still challenging - rough, technical terrain, heather bashing

## Context for Stephen
- Multi-day epic through the Scottish Highlands
