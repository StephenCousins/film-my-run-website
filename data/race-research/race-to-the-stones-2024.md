# Race to the Stones 2024 - Research

**Stephen's Race:** 13/07/2024 | 13:18:02 | 62mi (100km)

---

## Race Overview
- Organised by Threshold Sports (NOT Centurion Running)
- 100km along the Ridgeway - "Britain's oldest road"
- Lewknor (Oxfordshire) to Avebury Stone Circle (Wiltshire)
- 13th-14th July 2024
- Options: 100km non-stop, 100km over 2 days, 50km Day 1
- Over 90% completion rate - popular for first-time ultra runners
- The "stones" are the monolithic stones at Avebury (like Stonehenge)

## 2024 Weather Conditions
- "Weather was very mixed"
- Rain, mud, and challenge
- Some participants reported enjoying good weather "for the first time this year"
- Typical British summer conditions
