# South Downs Way 100 2018 - Race Report

**Source:** https://centurionrunning.com/reports/2018/2018-sdw100-race-report
**Date:** June 13, 2018
**Stephen's Result:** 24:44:57

---

## Race Overview
The seventh edition of the South Downs Way 100 took place on June 13, 2018, with exceptional conditions. A record field of 305 runners participated in what marked the organization's 10,000th starter across all events and their 27th 100-mile race.

## Weather & Conditions
The day "dawned warm and dry" with excellent weather persisting throughout the entire event, contributing to outstanding finishing statistics.

## Men's Race
Charlie Harpur, competing in his first 100-miler, dominated the men's competition. **Top Finishers:**
- 1st: Charlie Harpur (15:01)
- 2nd: Stellan Fries (15:50, MV50 record)
- 3rd: John Melbourne (15:56)
- 4th: Jon Ellis (first 100-miler, battled stomach issues)

## Women's Race
Sarah Cameron claimed victory in her debut Centurion event, running "strong and ahead from the gun." She built her lead progressively: 2 minutes at mile 22, 14 minutes by mile 35, and 25 minutes at mile 54. She finished in 18:14.

**Top Finishers:**
- 1st: Sarah Cameron (18:14)
- 2nd: Laura Swanton (18:45, second consecutive second-place finish)
- 3rd: Rebecca Lane (20:06)

## Notable Achievements
- 223 buckles awarded (highest ever for a 100-mile event)
- 73% finish rate
- 108 runners completed 100 miles in one day
- John Fanshawe set a new MV70 record of 25:11, improving the previous mark by three hours
- Ken Fancett won the MV60 category for the sixth time

## Volunteer Recognition
113 volunteers supported the event, with organizers acknowledging "the staggering amount of hours poured into" the production.
